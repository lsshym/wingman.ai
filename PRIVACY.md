# Privacy Policy

Wingman is a local AI coding plugin. The plugin itself does not collect, transmit, sell, or store personal data outside the user's local development environment.

Wingman may create or update project-local files such as `.wingman/memory/`, `.wingman/registry/`, `AGENTS.md`, `CLAUDE.md`, and `.cursor/rules/wingman-memory.mdc` when the user invokes the relevant skills.

Any data processed by the user's AI coding platform is governed by that platform's own privacy policy and settings.
